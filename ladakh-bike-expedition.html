Package Overview

Gear up for the ultimate adventure in the Himalayas. Our Ladakh Bike Expedition takes you through winding roads, high altitude passes including Khardung La, and the mesmerizing landscapes of Nubra Valley and Pangong Lake.

Itinerary Highlights

  • Day 1: Arrival in Leh & Acclimatization
  • Day 2: Leh Local Sightseeing (Magnetic Hill, Sangam, Shanti Stupa)
  • Day 3: Leh to Nubra Valley via Khardung La Pass
  • Day 4: Nubra Valley to Pangong Tso via Shyok River
  • Day 5: Pangong Tso to Leh via Chang La Pass
  • Day 6: Rest Day in Leh / Local Cafe Hopping
  • Day 7: Departure from Leh Airport
